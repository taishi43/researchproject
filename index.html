Anti-Asian Hate in Portland

I want to find information about the negative experiences lived by Asian people in the Portland Metro Area, and how the COVID 19 pandemic may have affected or exacerbated the frequency of those experiences. The term “Asian Americans” represents a diverse group of people with different cultural backgrounds who are often demographically grouped together which likely hinders the understanding of Asian Americans in the US. By illuminating the experiences of Asian people in the United States, I wish to improve the understanding of the diversity and difficulties of Asian people.
